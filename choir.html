Looks fun? Sign Up!

We are looking for:

  • Teens ages 12-20
  • Able to sing and follow music instructions.
  • Able to play musical instruments (can bring their own if they want).
  • Have confidence to lead and perform on stage.
  • Is a team-player.
  • Able to commit outside of school/studying times.

You must prepare the following:

  • For minors (aged 18 and below), a letter of consent from your guardian.
  • A photocopy of your school ID.
  • 2 pieces 2x2 picture in white background.

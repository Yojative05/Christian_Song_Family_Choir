About Us

We are an independent organization of choir members that service the Roman Catholic Churches in the Isle of Man.

We give praises to our Lord God through songs and music during the Holy Eucharist and on Special events.

Our Members

The Music team is comprised of singers and instrumentalist with varying age from 12 years of age up to 20 years old.

They come from different backgrounds of life and are dedicated for service in the Church.

At the moment we have 20 young and active singers and 10 instrumentalists.

Musical Instruments

We engage in different musical instruments, such as string, wind and percussion.

The members learn how to play the instruments as well as improve their voice.
